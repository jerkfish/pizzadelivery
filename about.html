OUR STORY

From a single store in 1960 to the world's leading pizza delivery company

WHO WE ARE

Founded in 1960, FornoExpress Pizza has grown from a single location in Ypsilanti, Michigan, into the largest pizza company in the world based on global retail sales. With more than 17,000 stores in over 90 markets, FornoExpress is a company of exceptional people on a mission to be the best pizza delivery company in the world.

Our fundamental business is pizza delivery, carried out by a network of dedicated corporate and franchise stores. We continue to focus on our core strengths of order-taking, pizza-making, and door-to-door delivery, constantly improving each element to provide better customer service.

At FornoExpress, we don't just make pizza. We make people happy. That's our mission, and it's what drives us to get better at what we do. From our chefs creating new recipes to our technologists building easier ordering systems, we're all about making the pizza experience better from start to finish.

OUR JOURNEY

1960

The Beginning

Brothers Tom and James Monaghan purchase "DomiNick's," a small pizza store in Ypsilanti, Michigan. James soon trades his half of the business to Tom for a used Volkswagen Beetle.

1965

FornoExpress Pizza is Born

Tom Monaghan renames the business "FornoExpress Pizza, Inc." The three dots in the logo represent the first three FornoExpress stores.

1978

200th Store Opens

FornoExpress opens its 200th store, showing rapid expansion across the United States.

1983

International Expansion

FornoExpress opens its first international store in Winnipeg, Canada, beginning global expansion.

1989

5,000th Store

FornoExpress opens its 5,000th store, becoming one of the fastest-growing pizza chains in the world.

1998

Online Ordering

FornoExpress launches its first website, allowing customers to order pizza online for the first time.

2004

Pizza Tracker

FornoExpress introduces the innovative Pizza Tracker, allowing customers to follow their order from preparation to delivery.

2010

Recipe Reinvention

FornoExpress launches a completely new pizza recipe with improved crust, sauce, and cheese, responding to customer feedback.

2025

17,000+ Stores

FornoExpress reaches over 17,000 stores worldwide, solidifying its position as the global leader in pizza delivery.
