Gluten-Free Options

FornoExpress offers a gluten-free crust for those with gluten sensitivities. However, please note:

  • Our gluten-free crust is prepared in a common kitchen with the risk of gluten exposure
  • For those with celiac disease or severe gluten allergies, we do not recommend our gluten-free pizza
  • The gluten-free crust is available in small size only

Gluten-Free Ordering Tips

  • Choose the gluten-free crust option when ordering online or in-store
  • Select gluten-free toppings (most vegetables and meats are gluten-free)
  • Avoid breadsticks, pasta, and sandwiches
  • Inform staff about your gluten sensitivity when ordering

Vegetarian Options

FornoExpress offers a variety of vegetarian options. All our cheese, crust, and sauce options are vegetarian-friendly.

Vegetarian Ordering Tips

  • Choose from our vegetable toppings: mushrooms, onions, green peppers, black olives, tomatoes, pineapple, jalape√±os, and spinach
  • All pizza crusts are vegetarian
  • Most pasta dishes can be made vegetarian (avoid meat toppings)
  • Breadsticks and cheesy bread are vegetarian
  • Ask for no pepperoni or other meat toppings

Lower Calorie Options

Make smarter choices with these lower-calorie menu items and customization options.

Lower Calorie Ordering Tips

  • Choose thin crust instead of hand-tossed or deep dish
  • Opt for light cheese or ask for half the normal amount
  • Load up on vegetable toppings instead of meat
  • Choose tomato-based sauces over creamy alternatives
  • Order a small size instead of medium or large
  • Consider a salad (when available) as a side instead of breadsticks

Lower Sodium Options

Reduce your sodium intake with these menu choices and customization options.

Lower Sodium Ordering Tips

  • Choose vegetable toppings over processed meats like pepperoni and sausage
  • Ask for light sauce or no added salt
  • Select thin crust which typically has less sodium than other crust options
  • Avoid extra cheese and salty seasonings
  • Be mindful of chicken items which can be high in sodium
