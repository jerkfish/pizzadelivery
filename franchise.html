FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much can I expect to earn as a FornoExpress franchisee? +

Earnings vary based on location, sales volume, and operational efficiency. According to our 2025 Franchise Disclosure Document, the average annual gross sales for FornoExpress stores in the U.S. was approximately $1.1 million. However, individual results may vary significantly.

How long does the franchise approval process take? +

The entire process typically takes 3-6 months from initial application to store opening. This includes the application review, financial verification, training, and site selection. The timeline can vary based on your location and readiness.

What training and support does FornoExpress provide? +

FornoExpress provides comprehensive training including:

  • 6-week training program at our World Resource Center
  • Hands-on store operations training
  • Ongoing field support from business coaches
  • 24/7 operational support
  • Marketing and advertising assistance
  • Technology and systems training
Can I own multiple FornoExpress locations? +

Yes, many of our franchisees own multiple locations. After successfully operating your first store for at least 12 months and meeting performance standards, you may be eligible to apply for additional locations. We offer development agreements for qualified candidates looking to open multiple stores.

What territories are available for new franchises? +

Territory availability changes regularly as markets develop. We're actively seeking franchisees in many growing markets across the United States. During the application process, our real estate team will work with you to identify suitable locations based on market analysis and your preferences.
